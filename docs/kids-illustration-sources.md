# Kids Illustration Asset Sources

These packs are suitable options for replacing current bird-centric visuals with broader kids-store illustrations.

## Candidate Sources

1. Storyset (by Freepik)

- URL: <https://storyset.com/kids>
- Notes: High-quality editable scenes in SVG/PNG. Confirm license tier and attribution requirement for commercial deployment.

1. Icons8 Ouch! Illustrations

- URL: <https://icons8.com/illustrations/style--cute-color>
- Notes: Good for mascot and section accents. Verify paid plan terms for production use.

1. unDraw

- URL: <https://undraw.co/illustrations>
- Notes: Free and open-source style, easy color customization for brand matching.

1. DrawKit

- URL: <https://www.drawkit.com/free>
- Notes: Includes playful character packs and UI-friendly illustration sets.

## Import Rules

- Keep original source URL and license notes for every imported asset.
- Export optimized SVG/WEBP variants before adding to public assets.
- Store selected files under public/assets/images/kids-pack/ and update config/storefront.php to switch globally.
